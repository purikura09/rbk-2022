MY STORY

My passion for art & design began in my early years. I went to the Art School in Geneva (HEAD) in 2005 and studied Visual Arts & Interior Design. After years of freelance jobs in graphic design, I started a training in Web design (Ifage) in 2014 and learned a whole new aspect in design and this is where my journey into UX/UI began. In 2018, I took a UX online training with Interaction Design Foundation in order to learn more about the user experience research techniques.
